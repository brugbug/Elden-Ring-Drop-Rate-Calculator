Elden Ring Drop Rate Calculator


Item Discovery

Item Discovery is a stat in Elden Ring that determines the likelihood of acquiring items dropped form defeated enemies.

Each individual item has its own unique "base" chance to drop (calculated at 100 Discovery).

This chance can be bolstered via certain stats and equipment


Drop Rate Formula

The formula for calculating the drop rate is like so:

x = Floor([Base Drop Rate] * 10 * ([Discovery Stat] / 100))

Drop Rate = (x * 100) / (1000 + (x - ([Base Drop Rate] * 10)))


More Information

More detailed information on item discovery can be found on the Elden Ring FextraLife website (https://eldenring.wiki.fextralife.com/Discovery)
